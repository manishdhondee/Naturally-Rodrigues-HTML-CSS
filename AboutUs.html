About Us







Pandanus weaving was introduced to Rodrigues by African slaves who followed a long-standing tradition of weaving all kinds of baskets to serve a variety of needs. It’s arguably a neglected art form: There aren’t that many basket weavers out there nor are there schools. Instead, it is a tradition passed from generation to generation.

The Artist

Anne Félicité became interested in the art 30 years ago.She had no idea that she’d be taking home so many accolades, and that it’d be the way she makes her daily living today. With further training over the years, she continues to apply her own vision—like a true artist
